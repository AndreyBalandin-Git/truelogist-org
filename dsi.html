02.07.2025

Truelogist Joins ICC DSI
Digital Trade Readiness Working Group

As part of the global ICC Digital Standards Initiative (DSI), Truelogist has joined the Digital Trade Readiness Working Group (DTRWG).

Our participation focuses on:

  • systematically engaging with current developments in digital trade,
  • presenting Truelogist’s institutional vision on logic-based execution and admissibility,
  • coordinating the development of the Truelogist Validation Machine with regulatory, technological, and operational realities of the industry.

Truelogist views this participation as a key institutional interface between our admissibility architecture and the active standards and initiatives shaping international trade. We are not offering a replacement — we are building the foundation for reproducible execution in a multi-jurisdictional and high-uncertainty environment.

Background

ICC DSI (Digital Standards Initiative) is a flagship program of the International Chamber of Commerce aimed at harmonizing digital standards in global trade. The DTRWG focuses on assessing ecosystem readiness and defining interoperable approaches to digital implementation.

Truelogist Validation Machine is a logic-based validation engine designed for institutional verification of execution in logistics, law, and digital trade.
