FIATA: Legal Validation for eFBL

FIATA is the legal owner and standard-setting body behind the electronic FIATA Bill of Lading (eFBL), a document of growing importance in digital trade workflows.

Truelogist is currently developing a dedicated validation engine designed to assess the legal and structural integrity of eFBL documents across jurisdictions and transaction layers.

This validation engine will allow stakeholders — from shippers to insurers — to independently verify eFBL authenticity, provenance, and cross-border consistency, using a logic-based framework without relying on proprietary APIs or external declarations.
